My Story

After graduating from my high school in Prescott Valley, Arizona, I joined the U.S Navy as a Master-At-Arms, or military police. I got a lot out of the 7 years in, but during the last 2 or so years I just wasn't feeling fulfilled and wanted to take a leap of faith and make a career change. I had always been intrigued by the software development world so I decided to resign from the military after my obligated service time was fulfilled and moved out to Salt Lake City, Utah with a friend in 2021 with the intention of going to a web dev oriented bootcamp in Lehi, Utah called Devmountain. Having already been through a military bootcamp, I was interested in this idea of fast-paced learning. I couldn't have been happier with the challenge and knowledge learned at this school, and fully reazling just how much I truly enjoy this line of work.
